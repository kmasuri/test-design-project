Michelle's Memoir

Fashion Foundation Family Career

She is the first African-American First Lady of the United States. As first lady, Michelle Obama served as a role model for women and worked as an advocate for poverty awareness, education, nutrition, physical activity, and healthy eating. She supported American designers and was considered a fashion icon. Over the years, while attending numerous presidential events she wore many different designers. For example: Sergio Hudson, Christy Rilling, Stella McCartney, Peter Dundas,Tanya Taylor, and many others.
