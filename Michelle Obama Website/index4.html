Michelle graduated in 1981 as class salutatorian from Whitney Young High School, She went to Princeton University in New Jersey, graduating cum laude in 1985. Michelle has an outstanding career, she went to law school and became an associate at the Chicago office of the law firm Sidley & Austin.

At the firm, she worked on marketing and intellectual property law. She held public sector positions in the Chicago city government as an Assistant to the Mayor, and as Assistant Commissioner of Planning and Development. Michelle also served as the Associate Dean of Student Services at the University of Chicago, where she developed the university's Community Service Center. She began working for the University of Chicago Hospitals, first as executive director for community affairs and as vice president for Community and External Affairs
